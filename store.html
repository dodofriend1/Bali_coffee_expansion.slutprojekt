our stores get your coffee

Bali's coffee culture has made its way to Scandinavia, with many cafes and roasters offering premium Balinese coffee. Experience the flavors of Bali while exploring the vibrant cities of Scandinavia.

Gothenburg

A charming coastal city with a vibrant art and music scene, famous for its seafood and coffee culture.

Helsinki

A design-forward city with a vibrant food scene, renowned for its sauna culture and stunning natural scenery.

Stockholm

A sophisticated and picturesque city, known for its historical landmarks, museums, and stunning architecture.

Oslo

A stunning city surrounded by fjords and mountains, offering a unique blend of nature, culture, and modern city life.

Malmö

A multicultural city with a diverse food scene, featuring beautiful parks and a lively nightlife.

Copenhagen

A colorful and lively city with a rich cultural heritage, known for its gastronomy, design, and cycling culture.
